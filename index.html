Green Tea Extract

**Green Tea ** Derived from the leaves of the Camellia sinensis plant, Green Tea Extract is renowned for its antioxidant properties. It contains compounds like catechins that are believed to play a pivotal role in weight loss by accelerating fat oxidation and boosting metabolism.
2

L-Carnitine

**L-** An amino acid derivative, L-Carnitine plays a crucial role in energy production. It facilitates the transport of fatty acids into the mitochondria, where they are burned to produce energy. This process is essential for muscle movement and overall function of the heart and brain.
3

Chromium

**** An essential trace mineral, Chromium is known to enhance the action of insulin, a hormone critical for the metabolism and storage of carbohydrate, fat, and protein in the body. It may also be involved in the regulation of appetite, reduction of food cravings, and control of fat and cholesterol metabolism.
4

L-Theanine

**L-** Found primarily in tea leaves, L-Theanine is an amino acid that is known for its calming effects. It can help reduce stress and anxiety, promote relaxation without drowsiness, and improve focus and attention. Additionally, it has been linked to various other health benefits, including improved sleep and enhanced immune function.
5

Chlorogenic Acid

**Chlorogenic ** Commonly found in green coffee beans, Chlorogenic Acid is a potent antioxidant. It is believed to slow the absorption of fat from food intake and also activates the metabolism of extra fat. Furthermore, it can influence blood sugar levels, potentially offering benefits for those with diabetes.
6

Vitamin B6

**Vitamin B6 (Pyridoxine Hydrochloride):** An essential vitamin, Vitamin B6 plays a role in neurotransmitter synthesis, hemoglobin production, and gene expression. It's vital for brain development and function and helps the body convert food into energy.
7

Vitamin B12

**Vitamin ** Crucial for nerve tissue health, brain function, and the production of red blood cells, Vitamin B12 plays a role in the metabolism of every cell in the human body. It's involved in DNA synthesis and energy production.
8

Chromium Chloride

**Chromium ** A form of the mineral chromium, Chromium Chloride is often used in dietary supplements. It's believed to help regulate blood sugar levels by improving the way our bodies use insulin.
9

Pyridoxine Hydrochloride

**Pyridoxine ** Another form of Vitamin B6, Pyridoxine Hydrochloride is essential for the breakdown of proteins, fats, and carbohydrates from foods. It's also involved in the production of neurotransmitters and red blood cells.
10

Epigallocatechin Gallate (EGCG)

**Epigallocatechin Gallate (EGCG):** A type of catechin found in green tea, EGCG is a powerful antioxidant. It's been researched for its potential role in weight loss, heart health, and as a protective agent against certain types of cancers.
